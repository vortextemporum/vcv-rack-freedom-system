## Context to Preserve

**Key Decisions:**
- Plugin type: Synth (Instrument)
- Core concept: Noise-based hi-hat synthesis with fixed resonance peaks for warmth
- MIDI triggering: Different notes for closed (C1) and open (D1)
- Choke behavior: Closed always chokes open with instant (<5ms) cutoff
- Synthesis: Filtered noise with static generation (no random variation)
- Velocity: Affects both volume and tone brightness
- Inspiration: CR-78/808 but warmer and less metallic
- Aesthetic: Studio Hardware (professional 19" rack unit with industrial precision)
